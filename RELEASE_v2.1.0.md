Prospectors Plus v2.1.0 — smart timing, X-pattern, new look

### ✨ New (in the new "Smart / experimental" tab — off by default)

- **Smart timing (auto-tune).** Turn it on and the macro watches its own miss
  rate — if shakes miss the water or landing needs nudges more than ~20% of the
  time, it nudges the relevant timing and keeps the change only if the miss rate
  improves (trial-and-error, with the steps shown in the log).
- **X pattern.** Walks back into the water on alternating 45° diagonals instead of
  straight back, so each pass covers new ground — helps when you keep falling
  short on a straight line. Forward to land stays straight.

### 🎨 Changed

- **New rustic look** — a warm charcoal-grey base with a coffee/brass gold accent
  and a subtle film grain, for a more rugged, cowboy feel.

### 🖥️ Update

Click **Update now** in the in-app banner, or download **ProspectorsPlusSetup.exe**
below. Settings, builds, and calibration carry over. The two new options are
experimental and off by default — switch them on and test, then tune.
